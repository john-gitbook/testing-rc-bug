# Core concepts

Hey there! Before you dive in, it helps to get comfortable with a few core ideas — they show up everywhere, so a little familiarity now saves you time later.

At a high level, everything you build lives inside a **workspace**, and each workspace holds one or more **projects**. **Permissions** control who on your team can view, edit, or deploy those projects, so you can collaborate with confidence.

Take your time exploring the pages in this section — we've tried to keep things friendly and jargon-free, and the Assistant is always around if you'd rather just ask a question.
